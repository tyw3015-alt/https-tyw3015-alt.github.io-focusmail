How It Works

From cluttered inbox to clear priorities in seconds

1
Install the extension

Add FocusMail to Chrome in one click from the Chrome Web Store. No account or sign-up required.

2
Click "Rank Emails"

FocusMail opens Gmail in the background, scans your recent emails, and analyses each one for urgency signals.

3
See your priorities instantly

Emails appear ranked in three colour-coded categories — Urgent, When You Can, and Lower Priority — with a clear suggested action for each.

4
Check them off as you go

Tick each email as you handle it. Switch to the To-do list view for a clean, focused task list ordered by urgency.
